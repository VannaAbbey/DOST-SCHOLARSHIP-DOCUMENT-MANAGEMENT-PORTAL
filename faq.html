Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is the purpose of this website?

This website is designed to facilitate the submission of PDF files for DOST scholarship requirements. Students can upload, view, and manage their submitted documents easily.

2. How do I upload a PDF file?

To upload a PDF file:

  • Go to the Student Dashboard.
  • Click on the "Select PDF to upload" button.
  • Choose the PDF file from your computer.
  • Click the "Upload PDF" button.

3. How many files can I upload? What are those?

You can only upload one (1) PDF file that contains the following:

  • Certificate of Registration (COR)
  • Informative Copy of Grades
  • DOST List of Documents Form

4. What is the format for the file name?

Lastname_Semester_YearLevel

5. What types of files can I upload?

You can upload PDF files only. Ensure that your file is in PDF format and named appropriately before uploading.

6. How do I know if my file has been successfully uploaded?

After uploading, you will see a confirmation message on the screen. You can also check the "Uploaded PDF Files" section on the Student Dashboard to see your submitted files and their status.

7. What do the different file statuses mean?

  • Accepted: Your file has been reviewed and accepted.
  • Processing: Your file is currently being reviewed.
  • Not Accepted: Your file was not accepted. Please check the requirements and try again.

8. Can I delete an uploaded file?

Yes, you can delete an uploaded file by clicking the "Delete PDF" button next to the file in the "Uploaded PDF Files" section.

9. What should I do if I uploaded the wrong file?

If you uploaded the wrong file, delete it from the "Uploaded PDF Files" section and upload the correct file.

10. How can I view my uploaded files?

Go to the "Uploaded PDF Files" section on the Student Dashboard to view your uploaded files along with their upload date and status.

11. Can I re-upload a file that was not accepted?

Yes, you can re-upload a file that was not accepted. Ensure that you follow the file naming conventions and requirements before re-uploading.

12. Why was my file not accepted?

Your file may not have been accepted due to incorrect format, naming issues, or other requirements not being met. Please review the requirements and try again.

13. Is there a limit to the number of files I can upload?

Yes, please collate your Certificate of Registration (COR), Informative Copy of grades, and DOST List of Documents form into one PDF file only.

14. How do I log out of the Student Dashboard?

Click on the "Exit" link in the top navigation bar to log out of the Student Dashboard.
